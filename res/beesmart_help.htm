Features:

Bee Smart™ Pollinator Gardener is your comprehensive handheld guide to select plants for pollinators specific to your area and can be used for monitoring, citizen science, gardening, plant restoration, and conservation.

Bee Smart™ works in conjunction with Catch Notes, one of the leading free journaling apps on Android and iPhone. You will be prompted to download it if you do not already have it installed. Capture plant information and add your own notes with text, photos, or voice recordings. View your journal on any phone, tablet or the web with automatic sync.
